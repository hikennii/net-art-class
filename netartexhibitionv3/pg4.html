They Rule

 

(2004) Josh On

A politically explosive net art installation that visually represents the interconnectedness of America's corporate power elite. The installation tracks the board members of large corporations and demonstrates how power flows through multiple networks of influence. In rendering the unseen connections visible, the piece precludes the concentration of corporate power and deconstructs the ideal of transparency in capitalist economies.
